Sustainable AI for Multidisciplinary Applications

25th to 27th April 2024

About the Conference

  Sustainable AI can be understood as having two branches, AI for Sustainability and Sustainability of AI (e.g. reduction of carbon emission and computing power). This Conference aims at addressing both the branches.

  MIT ADT School of Computing International Conference aims to bring UG. / PG students, academicians, research scholars, scientists and industry persons to a common platform to share, discuss, and nurture recent trends in the domain of Artificial Intelligence roles and influences in the multidisciplinary approaches to various fields of AR/VR, Big Data, Blockchain, Cloud Computing Edge Computing, Cyber Security and develop working relationships to explore solutions to real-world problem.

  It also provides the foremost interdisciplinary forum for scientists, engineers, and practitioners to present their latest research results, ideas, developments, and applications Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

Al for Big Data and Data Analytics Al for Sustainable Cities AI for Agriculture and Food Technology Al for Healthcare and Life Science Al for Metaverse and Cloud Computing Al for Education AI for Blockchain and Security
